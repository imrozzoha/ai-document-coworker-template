SAMPLE SERVICE AGREEMENT

Last Updated: January 2024

IMPORTANT: This is a sample document for demonstration purposes only.
It does not constitute legal advice or a binding agreement.

---

1. PARTIES

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between:
- Service Provider: Acme Solutions Pty Ltd ("Provider")
- Client: [Client Name] ("Client")

---

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Provider agrees to deliver the following services:
- Software development and consulting
- System integration and API development
- Ongoing technical support during business hours (9am–5pm AEST)
- Monthly progress reports and documentation

Services outside this scope require a written amendment to this Agreement.

---

3. PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Fees
The Client agrees to pay the agreed project fee as outlined in the attached Statement of Work (SOW).

3.2 Invoicing
Invoices are issued on the first business day of each month.
Payment is due within 14 days of the invoice date.

3.3 Late Payments
Overdue amounts accrue interest at 2% per month from the due date.
The Provider reserves the right to suspend services for accounts more than 30 days overdue.

---

4. CANCELLATION POLICY

4.1 Cancellation by the Client
The Client may cancel this Agreement by providing 30 days written notice.
Cancellation fees apply as follows:
- Cancellation with more than 30 days notice: No fee.
- Cancellation with 15–30 days notice: 25% of the remaining contract value.
- Cancellation with fewer than 15 days notice: 50% of the remaining contract value.

4.2 Cancellation by the Provider
The Provider may terminate this Agreement with 14 days written notice if:
- The Client fails to make payment within the agreed terms.
- The Client materially breaches this Agreement and fails to remedy within 7 days of written notice.

4.3 Immediate Termination
Either party may terminate immediately for cause, including fraud, gross misconduct, or insolvency.

4.4 Effect of Cancellation
Upon cancellation:
- All outstanding invoices become immediately due.
- Deliverables completed to date remain the property of the Client.
- Work in progress is returned or transferred as agreed in writing.

---

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 Client Ownership
All deliverables produced specifically for the Client under this Agreement become the Client's property upon full payment.

5.2 Provider Tools and Frameworks
The Provider retains ownership of any pre-existing tools, frameworks, or libraries used to develop the deliverables.
The Client is granted a perpetual, royalty-free licence to use these components as part of the deliverables.

---

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary or sensitive information shared during the engagement.
This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of 2 years.

---

7. LIABILITY

7.1 Limitation
The Provider's total liability under this Agreement is limited to the total fees paid by the Client in the 3 months preceding the claim.

7.2 Exclusions
The Provider is not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental losses.

---

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Disputes should first be raised in writing. If unresolved within 14 days, the parties agree to mediation before pursuing legal proceedings.
This Agreement is governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia.

---

9. AMENDMENTS

Any changes to this Agreement must be agreed in writing and signed by both parties.

---

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement, together with any attached SOW, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
