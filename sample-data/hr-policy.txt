1. INTRODUCTION

This Employee Handbook outlines the policies and expectations of Acme Solutions Pty Ltd ("the Company").
All employees are expected to read, understand, and comply with the policies in this document.

---

2. WORKING HOURS

2.1 Standard Hours
Standard working hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00am to 5:30pm AEST, with a 30-minute unpaid lunch break.
Flexible start times between 8:00am and 10:00am may be agreed with your manager.

2.2 Overtime
Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager. Approved overtime is compensated at 1.5x the standard hourly rate.
Overtime worked beyond 10 hours per week may be taken as time in lieu.

2.3 Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Core collaboration hours of 10:00am to 3:00pm must be maintained regardless of location.

---

3. LEAVE ENTITLEMENTS

3.1 Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 20 days (4 weeks) of paid annual leave per year.
Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance, except in emergencies.
Unused leave may be carried forward up to a maximum of 40 days. Balances above this threshold are paid out annually.

3.2 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days paid sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.
Unused sick leave does not carry forward or accumulate beyond 30 days.

3.3 Parental Leave
Primary carers are entitled to 16 weeks paid parental leave after 12 months of continuous employment.
Secondary carers are entitled to 4 weeks paid parental leave.
Leave must be requested at least 8 weeks in advance where practicable.

3.4 Compassionate Leave
Employees may take up to 3 days paid compassionate leave per occasion following the death or serious illness of an immediate family member.

3.5 Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all NSW public holidays as paid leave.
Working on a public holiday requires prior approval and is compensated at double time.

---

4. PERFORMANCE AND CONDUCT

4.1 Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to:
- Act with integrity and professionalism
- Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect
- Protect confidential company and client information
- Report conflicts of interest to their manager

4.2 Performance Reviews
Formal performance reviews are conducted annually in December.
Mid-year check-ins are held in June.
Performance improvement plans may be initiated where concerns are identified.

4.3 Disciplinary Process
Disciplinary action follows a progressive process:
1. Verbal warning
2. Written warning
3. Final written warning
4. Termination

Serious misconduct (e.g., fraud, harassment, safety violations) may result in immediate termination.

---

5. HEALTH AND SAFETY

The Company is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment.
All employees must:
- Report hazards, near-misses, and incidents promptly
- Complete mandatory workplace safety training
- Not attend work under the influence of drugs or alcohol

---

6. PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Client Data
Employees must not share, copy, or disclose client data outside approved systems.
Violations may result in disciplinary action and personal liability.

6.2 Company Information
Business strategies, financial information, and proprietary processes are strictly confidential.
This obligation continues for 2 years after employment ends.

---

7. TECHNOLOGY AND COMMUNICATION

7.1 Acceptable Use
Company devices and systems are for business use. Reasonable personal use is permitted.
Employees must not use company systems for illegal, offensive, or harmful activities.

7.2 Monitoring
The Company reserves the right to monitor use of company systems to the extent permitted by law.

7.3 Social Media
Employees must not share confidential business or client information on social media.
Personal views expressed online must be clearly distinguished from company views.

---

8. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE

Employees with workplace concerns should:
1. Discuss the issue with their direct manager.
2. If unresolved, escalate to HR in writing within 5 business days.
3. HR will investigate and respond within 10 business days.
4. If still unresolved, escalation to the CEO is available.

All grievances are treated confidentially.

---

9. AMENDMENTS

This Handbook may be updated periodically. Employees will be notified of material changes via company email.
